About me

Hi, I’m Ross. I am a web developer from Arizona, based in Shanghai. I have spent the last 4 years learning all things IT, but I specialize in web development with Javascript. Simplicity, reliability, and elegance are the guiding principles behind my work. I identify real-world problems, and solve them.

I’m good with HTML/CSS/Javascript/JQuery, Node/Express/NPM, React/Redux, Contentful,API integration as well as deployment/hosting. I have a significant amount of practice and course work with C, Java, shell scripting, and Linux sysadmin as well.

These days I am learning to be better with Postgresql, GraphQL, and Go, so I can build the API services that go along with my apps, and do more with my data. Full-stack web development will be included with Ross 2.0, coming soon to an internet near you!
