About me

Hi, I’m Ross. I am a web developer from Arizona, based in Shanghai. I have spent the last 4 years learning all things IT, but I specialize in web development with Javascript. Simplicity, reliability, and performance are the guiding principles behind my work.

I’m good with HTML,CSS,Javascript,JQuery, Node.js, React/Redux, VUe.js, headless CMS, API integration as well as deployment/hosting. I have a significant amount of practice and course work with C, Java, shell scripting, and Linux sysadmin as well.

I've been working with a digital agency in Shanghai for over year now, and have worked primarily with React doing marketing campaigns and company websites for big name clients in luxury, fashion, and gaming. In my free time I'm trying to get more experience with Go, Vue.js, PostgreSQL, and WeChat mini-program development.
